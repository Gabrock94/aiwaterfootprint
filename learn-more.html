AI Water Footprint Tracker

Understanding the environmental impact of AI models

What is AI Water Footprint?

Every time you interact with AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, data centers consume significant amounts of water for cooling servers and generating electricity. This "water footprint" represents the hidden environmental cost of AI interactions.

Did you know? A single ChatGPT conversation can consume up to 500ml of water - equivalent to a standard water bottle!

How Our Tracker Works

Detection

Automatically detects when you use AI services like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others.

Calculation

Estimates water consumption based on model type, query complexity, and data center efficiency.

Tracking

Provides daily summaries and insights to help you understand your AI water footprint.
