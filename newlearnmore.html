What is AI Water Footprint?

Training and running AI models requires massive computing power in large data centers. These servers generate significant heat, which must be cooled to avoid damage. One of the most common cooling methods uses water: chilled water circulates through cooling systems, absorbs heat, and then evaporates or is discharged.
